Project name: Evolve (2022)

Explanation: Anthropometric focussed school chair that is adjustable to all children from the age of 5 years to 12 years old.

Category: advanced ergonomic prototyping, educational furniture


Situation

Every student is different in terms of length and concentration. Active learning promotes concentration and focus. In addition, activity is good for the health and physical development of the child and a smart dynamic learning workplace supports this. Smart monitoring can help to learn from its use and interventions. The client in this situation is Royal Ahrend.

Task

To design a dynamic and smart learning environment for primary education for better learning performance and physical development. The main objectives are to design: for modularity (i.e. many configuration possibilities), in the chain (i.e. (re)use, circularity, production, simple and quick assembly), for adjustability (i.e. in height and depth and able for children to do it themselves), smart systems (i.e. equipped with sensors and looking at seating behaviour), and for personalisation.
