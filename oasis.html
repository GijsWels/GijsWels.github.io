A spot on the main square of Delft is transformed into a green oasis of coolness and tranquillity.
The oasis can be dismantled whenever there is a festivity or it is time for the weekly market.
Underneath the oasis is a water basin that fills itself during autumn and winter to water the trees and plants during the spring and summer.
The trees provide shadow to escape the sun.
Because of the built environment, the temperature keeps rising in the cities.
Greenery helps cooling down the built environment.

Urban Oasis (2021)

An adaptive green city square in fight against the urban heat island effect.
